Baldur Sigurðsson

I specialize in the topological study of singularity theory over the complex numbers, especially the relationship between surface singularities and low dimensional topology. Currently, I am interested in using tropical methods, extending methods which use Newton diagrams, studying the Lipschitz geometry of nonisolated singularities, as well as extending t√™te-√†-t√™te methods to higher dimensions.
